Our founding

In Janurary of 2022, Bootstats started out on Reddit with just a couple hundred users. Now it's a globally used product with millions of pageviews a month from 166 countries. Our goal is to provide insightful and actionable football/soccer stats to everyday users, websites, and companies. After all - what is data if we can't understand it? We're looking for people who can help us extend the service even further. We're looking to build mobiles apps, as well as marketing Bootstats on a bigger scale globally. Built with love for the Beautiful Game.

Growth & beyond

Bootstats provides free football widgets for other websites to use. These data widgets are viewed tens of thousands of times everyday at big websites such as OneFootball, JuveFC, and many other fan club websites. We also provide WordPress plugins that work right out of the box to let you integrate football tables and stats onto your website! Additionally, our Football Stats API let anyone create their own website or make their own prediction models.
